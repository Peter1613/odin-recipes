BBC Baked Breaded Chicken

Description:

No need to deep fry! Tasty fried chicken style dish without all that grease.

Ingredients:

All-purpose flour, 2 or 3 eggs, any brand of plain corn flakes, one each chicken parts: drumstick, thigh, breast, wing

Steps:

  1. Buy or have on hand: flour, eggs, corn flakes, chicken parts.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  3. Break eggs into a bowl, scramble. Put corn flakes in a separate bowl and crush them, and put flour into a third bowl.
  4. Wash the chicken and press both sides of each piece into flour.
  5. Dip each flour-coated chicken piece into the egg mixture.
  6. Press both sides of each egg-coated chicken piece into the corn flakes.
  7. Lay each piece on a baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  8. Open mouth and cram it in. (Disclaimer: Webmaster, author, owner, etc. is not responsible for choking if you actually do this and forget to chew.)
