RARE Rice-a-Roni with eggs

Description:

Yum! Add some extra protein to this awesome convenience food.

Ingredients:

Any flavor you like of Rice-A-Roni that you would enjoy with eggs, 2 or 3 eggs

Steps:

  1. Buy or have on hand: box of Rice-A-Roni and 2 or 3 eggs.
  2. Break eggs into a bowl and scramble.
  3. Prepare Rice-A-Roni according to package instructions.
  4. As soon as you complete above step and while the Rice-A-Roni is still steaming hot, pour in eggs and stir thoroughly. This will cook the eggs.
  5. Open mouth and cram it in. (Disclaimer: Webmaster, author, owner, etc. is not responsible for choking if you actually do this and forget to chew.)
